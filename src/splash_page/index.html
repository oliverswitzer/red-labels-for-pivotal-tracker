WWLTW Chrome Plugin

A simple tool to help you remember what you learned as you work.

Download it

No longer spend your Friday afternoon in a frantic state

Have you been asking yourself "what DID I learn this week?". This extension will help you remember to write down your week's learnings throughout the week as you finish your Pivotal Tracker stories.

Take advantage of using Markdown in your emails

No need to download special plugins to use markdown in your emails. Just "Finish" your WWLTW chore, and the plugin will auto-generate an email with your learnings, rendered in language-specific markdown
