What We Learned This Week for Pivotal Tracker

How to use the extension:

1

Enter Pivotal Tracker API token

WWLTW Chore in your icebox

The WWLTW for Pivotal Tracker extension uses your Pivotal Tracker API token to create a WWLTW chore for you each week at the top of the icebox. This chore is where we'll help you keep track of this week's learnings. You can find your API token right here on your Tracker user profile.

Token saved
2

Finish a project story

Enter a learning

Saving learnings all week means no more scrambling to remember them on Friday. As you "Finish" your Pivotal Tracker stories throughout the week, the extension will prompt you to record what you learned this week. We'll save your learnings for you in the weekly chore.

3

Finish the WWLTW story

Autogenerate WWLTW email

On Friday afternoon, the extension will ring a little alarm to remind you to share your learnings out with the WWLTW Google Group. Simply "Finish" the week's WWLTW chore and we'll craft your email in a new tab for you. Don't worry, you'll still have plenty of opportunity to polish, format and add a cute signature before the email gets sent.
